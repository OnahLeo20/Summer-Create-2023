SUMMER CREATE 2023

About Us

CFS Summer Create 2023 is a three (3) week program dedicated to developing and empowering the next generation of creative minds. Here learners are equipped with creative skills and aquire knowledge to be able to independently adapt to the positive extracurricular trends in the society. Our main goal is to give basic and expert knowledge to kids who are interested in aquiring life sustaining skills. This goal is achievable with our highly equipped and ever ready team of CFS and out-sourced staff as the case may be, to deliver these knowledge in all the fun way possible. We hope you enjoy your three weeks SUMMER CREATE 2023. Enjoy!!!
